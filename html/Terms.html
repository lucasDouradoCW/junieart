Things I will draw:

  • OCs
  • Fictional characters
  • Real people (with their consent)
  • Ship art (hugging, kissing, etc)
  • Pets of any kind - dogs, cats, horses, fish, birds, lizards, and anything else!

Things I won't draw (May change):

  • NSFW art
  • Pet portraits WITHOUT reference images provided
  • Mechs
  • Gore/heavy body horror

Important information:

  • Upon submitting your commission through the form in the "Commission Me" page, I will get back to you by email as soon as I can to confirm the details, then I will start the drawing.
  • I will ask for reference images and additional information when I contact you by email, so no need to send them when you submit the form!
  • If you're in Canada, payment will be done through eTransfer. Otherwise, payment is done through PayPal. I will send you the sketch before payment, and will continue drawing once full payment has been received.
  • You may cancel the commission at any time before the payment described above. It may not be possible to cancel after that.
  • The estimated delivery time on the form is just a rough estimate. I have often gotten commissions done much faster than that, but I could also take longer.
  • While it is very unlikely to happen, if you chose express delivery and I fail to get the commission to you within the timeframe I give, I will refund the amount you paid extra for faster delivery.
  • I will require reference pictures of the pet you want a portrait of - the more pictures, the better!
  • I have the right to refuse any commission if I am uncomfortable or unable to draw it for any reason.
