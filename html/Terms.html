Things I won't draw (May change):

  • NSFW art
  • Pet portraits without reference images provided

Important Information:

  • Your commission may take a week or two at most to be done. If you want it faster, I will charge more (15%) for a faster delivery!
  • I will require reference pictures of the pet you want a portrait of. More pictures are better, so I can represent your pet well!
  • For pet portraits, I am able to write a name and/or phrase on the page. Please let me know if and what you'd like asap! Examples here.
  • Also for pet portraits, I can use tinted or white paper. You can let me know if you have a preference or if I should decide! All colours can be seen here.
  • For characters, reference images are very much appreciated but not necessary. Without a reference, however, I will ask for extra (20%)!
  • If you don't have a reference for a character, please include as much information as possible.
  • Payment will be done through PayPal. I will send you the sketch, and will continue drawing after the full payment is sent.
  • I am currently in southern Ontario. For physical art, if you want the physical version, I am happy to send it to you! I will ask for enough to cover shipping, and will package the piece up safely to ship! I will also send you a scanned copy.

If you agree to all these, go ahead and contact me here!
